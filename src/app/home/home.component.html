About Our Farm

Project Jungle is a service provider for creating natural, wild, maintenance free, native forests. At Project Jungle, we work passionately on and off-site to provide the best solutions at the lowest possible cost. Started in September 2018, Project Jungle is a social enterprise on a mission to bring back our native forests that have been lost. We do it by creating them.

Consultation

Large scale forestation

Type of forest extends over a large area. It can be any waste land. This type of forest can be of timber, fruit, bee or bird attracting, medicinal forests.
